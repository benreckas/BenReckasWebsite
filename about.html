About

My wife, Norma, and I have been married for 5 years and currently live in Clovis, CA. We have two daughters together, Olivia and Shoshanna. I try to spend as much time as I can with the girls. This usually means crawling around our living room with a toddler on my back insisting, ”No, you’re not Daddy! You’re a horse”.

When I am not coding- I enjoy music, movies, design, building things, video games, and the occasional book (you know, when the internet stops working).

I started programming and developing because it is incredibly interesting and challenging. I enjoy the strategy, logic, and problem solving that are at the core of programming. Most of all, I love creating websites that make the interaction between the user and the content special in some way. If you'd like to build something together, I'd love to hear from you.
